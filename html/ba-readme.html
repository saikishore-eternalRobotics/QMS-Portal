Business Analyst Role

Role Summary

The Business Analyst (BA) is responsible for understanding business needs, documenting requirements, and ensuring that the development team clearly understands what needs to be built. The BA acts as a bridge between the business team and the technical team.

Main Responsibilities

  • Gather and understand business requirements.
  • Create and update documentation like BRD, FRD, and user stories.
  • Communicate with stakeholders to clarify requirements.
  • Add detailed requirements and notes to ClickUp tasks.
  • Coordinate with developers, QA, and PMs for clarification and testing.
  • Support UAT (User Acceptance Testing) and ensure all requirements are met.

Daily / Weekly Tasks

  • Update ClickUp tasks with clear requirements and comments.
  • Track task progress and dependencies.
  • Attend meetings with stakeholders and the project team.
  • Review development progress and raise clarifications when needed.
  • Verify completed work against requirements before closure.

Deliverables

  • Business Requirement Document (BRD)
  • Functional Requirement Document (FRD)
  • User Stories / Acceptance Criteria
  • Process Flow Diagrams or Notes
  • UAT Support Sheet

Workflow

  1. Collect requirements from stakeholders.
  2. Document and upload details in ClickUp.
  3. Review requirements with tech and QA teams.
  4. Get approval or sign-off.
  5. Support testing and confirm that deliverables meet business needs.
  6. Close tasks once approved.

Communication

  • Use ClickUp comments for all requirement clarifications.
  • Tag relevant people (PM, Dev, QA) when action is needed.
  • Keep all documentation links updated inside the ClickUp task.

Best Practices

  • Keep requirements clear and structured.
  • Always update ClickUp when any change is made.
  • Follow naming conventions for tasks and documents.
  • Maintain traceability between requirement → task → UAT.
  • Review and confirm before marking tasks as complete.
